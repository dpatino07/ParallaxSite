Athletics with The Zom

About

The Zom athletic training style is a unique, intensive, custom-taylored and results-driven program that focuses on building better athletes. The program is divided into 4 phases of training: functional, strength & conditioning, technique, and performance.

Programs

Train Unique, Train Right, Train Like a Beast

Programs will be led by Dom who has over 10 years of experience in the fitness industry and has developed/designed workout programs for over 200 clients. Dom is a certified personal trainer with strong specializations in athletic movement & conditioning. The programs will be designed based on a day 1 assessment and will be designed to meet the client goals using The Zom Athletic program phases.

    Common Programs Include:

  • Spartan/OCR Training
  • Calisthenic/Ninja Warrior Training
  • Daily Athlete Lifestyle
